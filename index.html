Biography

I am currently a third-year Ph.D student in Department of Computer Science in the University of Massachusetts Lowell. My supervisor is Dr. Yu Cao. Prior that, I received my B. Eng degree from Computer Science and Technology department in 2011 from Huazhong University of Science and Technology.

My research interest includes computer vision and machine learning, especially, detection and localization. I'm interested in applying various deep learning techniques to distributed system using mobile devices. Specifically, I'm curious about how to use Convolutional Neural Network(CNN) for object detection in medical images. Recently I'm doing research on Tuberculosis(TB) detection in chest X-ray images, calorie estimation in food images. We're developing fantastic systems using cloud server and edge devices for faster diagnosis and accurate detection.
